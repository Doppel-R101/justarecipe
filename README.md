**Create: JustARecipe**

Create: JustARecipe is an exciting addition to the Create mod, introducing a variety of new and balanced recipes to enhance your gameplay experience. Whether you're looking to streamline your production lines or discover alternative crafting methods, this datapack has something for you.

### Features

- **Innovative Recipes**: Discover new ways to craft and process items with carefully balanced recipes.
- **Log Processing**: Wash logs to obtain their stripped variants effortlessly.
- **Resource Crushing**: Crush flint to gain a chance of obtaining coal and gunpowder.
- **Dye and Obsidian Mixing**: Combine red dye and powdered obsidian to create redstone.
- **Bone Compaction**: Compact bonemeal into bones for efficient storage and use.
- **Slime Ball Creation**: Mix water with mud, sugarcane, lime dye, and/or dough to produce slime balls.
- **And More**: Explore additional recipes that expand your crafting possibilities.

### Compatibility

- **Minecraft Version**: Currently supports versions from 1.17.2 to 1.21.3(not listed in version releases)
- **Mod Platforms**: Available as a datapack and as a mod (JAR) for Forge, Fabric, NeoForge, and Quilt.

### Installation

- **Datapack**: Add the datapack to your world's datapacks folder.
- **Mod (JAR)**: Download the JAR file and place it in your mods folder.
